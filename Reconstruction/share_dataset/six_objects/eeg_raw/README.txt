The eeg experiment was design following procedur

- There are 6 classes of object, and each class consis of 20 
images
- Stimulus time was shown for 5 second with inter trial interval 0.3 second (black screen)
- Following fixation cross shown 0.5 second between trial
- 10 trial / block, total 12 block (total 120 images)
- Rapid-design experiment
- No two consecutive class

ex:

0.5s   0.3s             0.5s            0.5s
tiger  black_screen     fixation_cross  next_stimulus
